🙋‍♂️ About Me

Hello, I’m Cristian Ávila, an Environmental Engineer with a background in EHS and sustainability. In my previous roles within the construction and industrial sectors, I often faced the challenge of turning large volumes of operational and environmental data into actionable insights.

Realizing the power of data to drive meaningful change, I upskilled in data analytics—learning Python, SQL, Power BI, and predictive modeling. I applied these skills to analyze key performance metrics, build dashboards, and support compliance and risk mitigation strategies in EHS operations.

As a result, I was able to improve reporting efficiency, identify critical environmental trends, and support decision-making processes with clear and impactful visualizations.

Now, I’m focused on leveraging this blend of environmental expertise and data science to work on projects that tackle climate change and promote sustainable development. I’m also open to applying my analytical skills in dynamic fields like sports analytics, esports, gaming, and anime—where data is shaping the future of entertainment and fan experiences.
